Introducing: Tucker

Hi, I'm Tucker!

I am an aspiring web developer currently living in New Jersey.

I'm currently in the process of a career change! I've spent the past 4 years working as a Behavior Analyst (BCBA). Though it can be rewarding work, web development has always been the road I regret not taking. As a professional, I consider myself a team player, an effective communicator, and persistent when given a goal. I am enthused to be pursuing a career as a Software Engineer, and to grow as a professional through collaboration with others!

I'd love to hear from you!

About Me:

I am currently a student enrolled in Thinkful's Software Engineering Immersion Program. My passion for web design is fueled by the ability to make a difference, which is similar to what has motivated me professionally for my entire career. With the world becoming increasingly more digital, I feel web design is the perfect opportunity to make a difference. It is exciting to create a tangible product which has the potential to reach a large number of people.

Working on a team is a great way to facilitate growth personally and professionally, to learn new skills, and to create the best possible quality of work. I look forward to all that I can do as part of a team when combining my strengths with others strengths to create something great!

Outside of coding I enjoy spending time with family and friends, exploring new places to eat/drink, traveling, boating, skiing, music, guitar/singing, and playing video games!
